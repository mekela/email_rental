You can now live in Miami NeoFuturistic Palace called 1000 Museam

One Thousand museum is a new condo development that blends dramatic grandeur with a futuristic aesthetic. Designed by world reknowned, Pritzker Prize winning architect Zaha Hadid, this ultra luxurious 60 story tower dazzles the Miami skyline as a glass atrium intercepted by gracefully curving columnar bands. Located across Museum Park on Biscayune Boulevard, its in the midst of the bustle, art, culture and commerce of downtown Miami.

Inside, the building offers 83 residences, consisting of one to two units per floor. Half floor residences range from 4,600 to 4,755 sq ft with 10 foot ceilings. Full floor residences start from 9,900 sq ft and have 12 foot ceilings. There are also 4 duplex townhouse residences and one duplex full floor penthouse. Each unit has a terrace with 360 degree views of Biscayne Bay and the Atlantic Ocean. The units are finished with luxurious touches that include Gatto cucine kitchens, kreon lighting, and automation technology byCrestron.

One thousand museum proffers its residents with world class amenities, treating them like the contemporary equivalent of royalty. The tower has a private helipad, enabling residents to travel at their own convenienceand leisure, as well indoor and outdoor spas to pamper them from head to toe. No detail has been overlooked in assessing both the physical and emotional comfort of its residents. The building houses an aquatic center.
